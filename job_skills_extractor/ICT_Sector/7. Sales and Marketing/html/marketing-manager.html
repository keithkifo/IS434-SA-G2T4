Marketing Manager

Job Role Description

The Marketing Manager contributes to the realisation of the organisation’s business strategies by driving its marketing and integrated marketing communications (IMC) strategy, developing go-to-market product positioning strategy, generating data-driven insights and monitoring budgets. He/She develops roadmaps for new or enhanced products and services, determines product pricing and is responsible for the development and curation of offline and digital content for accuracy and relevancy. He analyses effectiveness and return on investment from partnership marketing to determine renewal of partnerships.

He works in a fast-paced, dynamic and digitally-centric environment where he is expected to lead the development of appealing marketing concepts to promote the organisation and its products. 

He is an innovative, energetic, collaborative and highly adaptable team leader. He is digitally-savvy and possesses a strong business acumen, strong interpersonal skills and a high level of initiative.
